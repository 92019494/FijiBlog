About Us

Fiji is an amazing place with beautiful colourful landscapes throughout the country. It also has many villages that allow tourists to fully immerse themselves in the culture. Fiji is a place that should be visited by people at least once in their lifetime. We think Fiji is such a great country and that's why we created this blog based web app that lets people share their experiences, tips and reccommendations from their time in Fiji. We aim to spread the word about all Fiji has to offer. We allow anyone and everyone all around the world to post their experiences on our blog.
